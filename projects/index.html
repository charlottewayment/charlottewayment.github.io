Projects

A selection of projects in academic and professional contexts.

Ongoing

Ongoing · AIMS CDT, Oxford Robotics Institute
Characterising Control Roles in Multimodal Biosignals
Characterising the control-relevant properties of multimodal biosignals to build a principled framework for when each modality should be trusted, down-weighted, or overridden in assistive device control.

Academic

MSc Thesis · Durham University · 2025
Groundwater Forecasting in Sparse Sensor Networks
Hybrid GAT-LSTM architecture for predicting groundwater levels at ungauged locations, achieving median KGE 0.676 across a rigorous leave-one-station-out protocol — with performance stratification by bedrock flow type providing a principled account of where and why the model fails.
MA Thesis · University of Edinburgh · 2024
Urban-Scale Modelling for a Net-Zero Scotland
Feature-based intersection clustering of Edinburgh's domestic building stock into 12 retrofit typologies, mapped spatially across the city and linked directly to existing government funding schemes via a resident-facing eligibility prototype.
Academic · University of Edinburgh HYPED Team · 2024
Hyperloop Simulations Engineering
Multi-scale CFD and solid mechanics simulations to identify high-risk failure points in a Hyperloop prototype, reducing manufacturing waste by over 30% through simulation-informed design iteration.

Professional

Professional · Wolfwyse Ltd. · 2021–2025
Production Scheduling & Optimisation Systems
Production-grade scheduling and optimisation for multinational food and pharmaceutical manufacturers (incl. MARS, GSK), combining constraint-based methods and evolutionary algorithms to reduce multi-day planning tasks to minutes in safety-critical production environments.
Professional · J P Hildreth Ltd. · 2018–2023
Facility Modelling for Regulated Manufacturing
Quantitative spatial and operational modelling for regulated food and pharmaceutical manufacturing facilities (incl. GSK, Mars Chocolate), translating complex facility constraints into prioritised production scenarios.
