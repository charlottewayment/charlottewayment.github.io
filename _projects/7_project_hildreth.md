**Role:** Project Engineer & Technical Support (Full-Time 2018–20; Contractor 2021–23)  
**Category:** Professional

As a project engineer and technical support consultant at J P Hildreth Ltd., I developed quantitative models of spatially and operationally constrained manufacturing systems for clients in highly regulated food and pharmaceutical environments, including GSK and Mars Chocolate.

The work involved translating facility-specific manufacturing conditions into priority-driven production scenarios — combining CAD-based spatial modelling with applied optimisation to evaluate layout and workflow trade-offs. A requirement throughout this work was communicating technically complex scenario analyses to both engineering and non-technical client teams, a skill I believe is inceredibly important in both professional and academic settings.

More generally, this role provided me with early experience in structured, constraint-aware modelling in settings where regulatory compliance and operational safety are hard requirements rather than preferences or desires — an early grounding in the gap between systems that perform well under idealised conditions and those robust enough to be trusted in practice.
