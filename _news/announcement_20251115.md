**Award Nomination:** I am happy to announce that my master's dissertation has been nominated for the UK Geologist's Association Curry MSc Prize 2026 - awarded for the best MSc Thesis in an earth sciences application in the UK. Exploring long-term groundwater dynamics, the project used graph attention and long short term memory networks to facilitate sustainable resource management in sparse and ungauged UK catchments.
