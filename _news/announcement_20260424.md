**Award Confirmation:** I was awarded the inaugural Trinity College Oxford Marconi-Moseley Postgraduate Research Prize for my work on biosignal decoding for robust, uncertainty-aware assistive device control. The judges highlighted a “shared willingness to question established practices a field has come to accept as settled, and to redefine progress” as a key strength of the work — an approach that continues to motivate and inform my research.
